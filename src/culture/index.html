Working at MDG

We do fun team building events, host conferences, host meetups, get dinner together, go on trips, etc. Every quarter(?) we have everyone on our team fly to SF headquarters, usually for some sort of launch, and we plan team events while everyone is in town.

Global Team

We look into various aspects of our culture when thinking about inclusiveness, one in particular is how we think about our remote team members. We use the latest systems possible to connect our remote employees: Collaboration apps like Slack and Dropbox Paper, VPN-less access to all systems, high-quality videoconferencing and top-of-the-line AV equipment. We strongly believe that face to face interaction is a key part of building a strong team, so we’ve put together a travel calendar for our remote team members to come to HQ to work and celebrate with the SF based team 2–4 times a year.

Benefits & Perks

Our team is our family – we work hard to take care of each other. Among many other benefits, we provide healthcare for you and your family, a healthy lunch and dinner, a flexible vacation policy, and a monthly stipend to cover housecleaning and gym memberships for you and a partner.

Healthcare
We offer a selection of health, dental and vision coverage. The company covers 100% of the cost of your own health benefits and 50% of the cost for spouses, domestic partners and children.
Flexible location
Work wherever you want. Just get great results.
Time off
We don't track vacation days: it's up to you to decide how much to take. The reason? Our only legitimate concern is how much you get done. We also offer fully paid parental leave!
Meals
You'll never go hungry! Meals are a community affair with us. We provide lunch, dinner and a kitchen fully stocked with healthy snacks and drinks for team members and their guests.
401K
We offer a 401(k) plan that all full-time employees are eligible to participate in starting as soon as you join.
Wellness stipend
We give everyone a flexible $500 stipend to cover fitness for yourself and a partner, house cleaning, and phone expenses.
Team and family events
We welcome you and your family to join us for company outings, happy hours, and game nights.
Life & disability insurance
We cover life insurance as well as short- and long-term disability coverage to provide peace of mind to you and your families.
And more...
In-office massages, coffee walks, yoga classes with an on-site teacher, a quiet and spacious office, ergonomic equipment...
