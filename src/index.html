Who we are

We are an open-source company dedicated to helping developers build great apps. All of us here want to make software development easier, better, and accessible to more people. What we do

We build Apollo, a family of tools that exists to support developers using GraphQL. Our open-source client, Apollo Client, is the most popular GraphQL client for React and mobile apps, our performance monitoring tool, Optics, is the only APM tool built specifically for GraphQL, and is a crucial tool for developers running GraphQL in production. We're proud of the partnerships we've made with other companies and thought leaders to grow GraphQL together.

We build Meteor, the most popular full-stack framework for building real-time apps with modern JavaScript, and Galaxy, the premiere hosting provider and performance monitoring solution for Meteor apps.
