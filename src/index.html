What we do

We build Apollo, a family of tools that enable developers to get the most our of GraphQL. Apollo Client is the most popular GraphQL client for React and mobile apps. Optics, our performance monitoring service, is the only APM tool specifically for GraphQL, and is crucial for developers running GraphQL in production. We work hard to grow GraphQL as a technology and partner with open source thought leaders and companies building on GraphQL.

We build Meteor, the most popular full-stack framework for building real-time apps with modern JavaScript, and Galaxy, our hosting and performance monitoring solution for Meteor apps. Thousands of companies, and hundreds of thousands of developers, have built their products on top of the Meteor framework.
