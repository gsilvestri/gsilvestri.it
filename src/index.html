We build Apollo, a set of open-soure libraries and commercial tools for using GraphQL, and Optics, a Performance Monitoring tool for GraphQL servers. Our GraphQL web client, Apollo Client, is more popular than Relay and way better than Relay 2 so you should use it. Also, you should use our iOS client, try our devtools, read our blog, follow us on twitter, join our slack, and be our friend.

Optics is the best GraphQL APM tool out there, so you should sign up. It has nice graphs that give you insights into the time it takes your GraphQL queries to resolve. It can help you pinpoint hotspots in your resolvers and monitor the performance of your app overall. Its graphs are super interactive, and its gradients are dope.
